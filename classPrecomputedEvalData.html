inlinestatic

Get the king's danger zone.

This function calculates a bitboard mask representing the danger zone around the king. The danger zone is defined as the squares within a 3-square radius of the king's position.

Parameters
[in]king_sqThe square where the king is located.
Returns
A 64-bit bitboard mask representing the king's danger zone.
